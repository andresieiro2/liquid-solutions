The Flexitank Service

We perfected the flexitank concept, and though we have our imitators, we still lead the field by some distance.

  • NOT ALL FLEXITANKS ARE THE SAME

    The Liquid Solutions difference starts even before we create your flexitank. We design the flexitank around the consignment to be carried. We never adopt a one size fits all policy.

  • PEACE OF MIND

    The Liquid Solutions flexitank story begins before its manufacture in sterile conditions. Our full traceability extends to beyond the flexitank being emptied of its taint free cargo and covers its complete recycling.

  • INDUSTRIAL FLEXITANK

    The Liquid Solutions Industrial flexitank is trusted by the world’s leading brands to carry chemicals, oils, lubricants, enzymes and many other non-hazardous liquids.

  • WINE FLEXITANKS

    Over the years we’ve perfected flexitanks which effectively eliminate the chance of tainting. As a result, the wine industry demand Liquid Solutions flexitanks to move their precious cargos across the world.
